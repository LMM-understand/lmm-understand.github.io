Overview


The emergence of large multi-modal models (LMMs) has revolutionized the field of computer vision and natural language processing. These models can understand and generate content across different modalities, including text, images, and videos. However, evaluating the capabilities and limitations of these models presents unique challenges. This tutorial provides a comprehensive exploration of the evaluation methods for large multi-modal models, addressing both technical challenges and practical considerations. We welcome students, researchers, and practitioners interested in understanding how to rigorously assess LMMs, identify their strengths and weaknesses, and develop more robust evaluation frameworks for the future generation of multi-modal AI systems.
